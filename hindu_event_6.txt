The Vedas are the oldest sacred texts in the Hindu religion, the word comes from the Sanskrit root, "vid" meaning "to know" The Vedas were passed from generation to generation orally, and are therefore known as "Sruthi," meaning, "what is heard"

There are 4 Vedas, Rig Veda, Yajur Veda, Sama Veda, Atharvaveda each describing various ritual practices as well as philosophical treatises. 

Veda is recited in every major Hindu ritual, and should be performed by those that are adequately trained and practiced in chanting.
